Spaghetti and tomato

A delicious plate of spaghetti

If you are a pasta lover like me, you will surely love this fantastic dish.

The original italian dish: spaghetti with tomato

Now let's learn how to make them!

Ingredients:

  • 100g of spaghetti
  • 100g of tomato ciliegino
  • One clove of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmigiano regiano

Steps:

  1. Fill a pan with water and make it boil, add salt and put the pasta
  2. Cut the tomatoes in 4 pieces
  3. Take a cooking pan, put a spoon of oil in it
  4. Take the garlic, squash it, put it on the cooking pan and make it brown
  5. Put the tomatoes in the cooking pan, add a bit of salt and pepper and make them cook until they become soft, then they are ready
  6. When the pasta is cooked, put it in the cooking pan with the tomatoes, add a bit of the pasta water and
    and mix all with low flame. You can remove the garlic at this point
  7. Put a bit of Parmigiano Regiano on it and the dish is ready
