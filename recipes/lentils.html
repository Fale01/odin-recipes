Soup of lentils

A plate of lentils

A perfect dish for cold days

Ingredients:

  • 100g of lentils
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Put some oil in the pan, add the cutted onion and make it brown
  2. Add the cutted carrots and potatoes, and make them fry a bit
  3. Add the lentils and, after making them fry a bit, add fresh water
  4. Make the lentils cook until they are ready, then add salt and pepper and serve them
