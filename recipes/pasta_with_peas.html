Pasta and peas

A delicious plate of pasta with peas

A fantastic dish, rich of taste and perfect for a healty meal

Ingredients:

  • 100g of pasta
  • peas
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmigiano regiano

Steps:

  1. Fill a pan with water and make it boil, add salt and put the pasta
  2. Take a cooking pan, put a spoon of oil in it
  3. Cut the onion, put it in the cooking pan and make it brown
  4. Add the peas, make them fry a bit, add salt and pepper and then fresh water. Make them cook until they are soft
  5. When the pasta is cooked, put it in the cooking pan with peas, add a bit of the pasta water
    and mix all with low flame
  6. Put a bit of Parmigiano Regiano on it and the dish is ready
